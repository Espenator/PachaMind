# PachaMind.org — Education Content Classification Matrix

**Purpose:** Define which content buckets are publishable, gated, or require counsel review before indexing. This matrix governs all curriculum production decisions for PachaMind.org.

**Governing constraint:** 100% free and nonprofit. No pricing, checkout, reservation pressure, fundraising, or sales language anywhere. No health, medical, therapeutic, or outcome claims. Puma Path is framed strictly as a learning progression, not initiation. All Huachuma/San Pedro content is academic/historical/cultural literacy only — never consumption guidance — and must not appear on the public front door.

## Risk Tier Reference

| Tier | Meaning | Required actions |
|---|---|---|
| **Low** | Cultural, educational, reflective content with no health claims | Publish freely; maintain brand.disclaimer on every lesson |
| **Medium** | Content that could be misread as prescriptive, proprietary, or health-adjacent | Review language carefully; add academic framing; verify attribution and rights |
| **HIGH** | Content with legal, regulatory, health, or safety exposure | Do NOT publish on public front door; require counsel review before indexing; keep out of sitemap and public nav |

## Per-Bucket Publication Checklist

### ✅ PUBLIC — Andean Cosmovision (Low)
- Cornerstone pages explaining the Andean worldview as cultural learning
- Three Pachas overview (Hanaq, Kay, Uku) as curriculum spine
- Kausay Pacha (living universe) framing
- **Must include:** brand.disclaimer on every lesson page

### ✅ PUBLIC — Three Pachas (Low)
- Individual lessons for Hanaq Pacha, Kay Pacha, and Uku Pacha
- Puma Path exercises presented only as learning progression (Semilla → Puma)
- **Must NOT:** use initiation language, ceremony booking language, or outcome promises

### ✅ PUBLIC — Five Andean Values (Low)
- Values pages and micro-lessons: Munay, Yachay, Llank'ay, Sami, Hucha
- Badge/reflection prompts tied to the Puma Path learning progression
- **Must include:** Mallku Aribalo attribution

### ✅ PUBLIC — Archaeo-Astronomy (Medium)
- Academic and historical articles on Andean sky-watching and calendar systems
- Site-based learning journeys (e.g., Machu Picchu, Sacsayhuamán alignments)
- **Must NOT:** claim predictive, astrological, or mystical powers; frame all content as scholarly and historical interpretation
- **Must include:** source citations and academic framing language

### ✅ PUBLIC — Mallku's Works (Medium)
- Author bio, teacher attribution, and book-theme summaries
- Deeper-study prompts referencing specific works
- **Must NOT:** reproduce extended excerpts without verified permission
- **Must include:** explicit credit to Mallku Aribalo as tradition-holder and source

### 🔒 GATED — Huachuma / San Pedro (HIGH)
- Historical, ethnobotanical, and cultural-literacy content only
- Accessible only after login/account creation
- **Must NOT:** appear in public nav, sitemap, library filter, or search results
- **Must NOT:** include any dosage, preparation, sourcing, or consumption guidance
- **Must NOT:** make therapeutic, medical, or experiential outcome claims
- **Must flag:** every page for counsel review before indexing
- **Framing required:** "This content is offered strictly as academic and historical cultural literacy. It does not constitute guidance of any kind."

### 🔒 GATED + COUNSEL — In-Person Peru Experiences (HIGH)
- Available only after Phase 2 user-screening pathway is implemented
- High-level description of 'screened Peru-only pathway' only
- **Must NOT:** include pricing, booking, reservation pressure, or dates
- **Must NOT:** appear publicly before counsel and ops review is complete
- **Requires:** counsel review + ops review before any public mention
