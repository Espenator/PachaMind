## Purpose

Give counsel and advisors a single, concrete operating model — entity roles,
content firewall, data/consent flows, communication channels, payment/revenue
framing, conversion definitions, and launch gates — so they can confirm the
structure before Flow A signup goes live, before any WhatsApp educational
messaging, and before any Flow B commercial activation.

## 3. Data and consent flow

During Flow A, PachaMind.org may collect **membership coordinates** for a
**free, gamified, two-way learning membership** — used for educational
membership administration only (learner progress, account access, reminders,
private reflections, quizzes, badges, Puma Path progression, language/region
relevance, and optional two-way educational communication), subject to counsel
review. This is **not** a passive newsletter or a hidden commercial lead funnel.

Approved Flow A membership fields: first name, last name, email, country/region,
country code, cell/WhatsApp number, preferred language, and **separate
communication permissions**.

**Best-case operating determination for counsel:** the legal/compliance risk is
**not** the collection of reasonable membership coordinates itself. The risk is
using those coordinates for **undisclosed Embodier.ai commercial marketing, paid
membership promotion, screening, payment, Peru pathway sales, or unrelated
communications before separate consent and counsel-approved Flow B.** Counsel is
therefore asked to focus on consent wording, consent logging, data-sharing
boundaries, channel consent, and the PachaMind/Embodier consent boundary —
rather than on a yes/no decision about whether phone or coordinates may be
collected for the real educational product.

Required consent architecture (separate, never bundled): free membership /
account-related messages; optional educational email; optional educational
WhatsApp; optional educational SMS only if activated later; and **no Embodier.ai
commercial consent during Flow A**.

Communication channels:

- Flow A: **email** (primary) and **optional WhatsApp** educational communication. **No paid SMS.**
- Flow B: mobile-first **WhatsApp and cell SMS before email** for marketing/sales — only after separate commercial consent and counsel-approved gates.

Consent firewall: a PachaMind.org free membership signup is **never** automatic
Embodier.ai commercial consent.

## 4. Payment and revenue framing

- Flow A: **no** donations, donor funnel, conservation contribution funnel, paid
  membership, paid upgrades, pricing, or payment forms.
- Future revenue is framed as **PachaMind membership and membership levels**,
  not donations.
- Paid membership levels are introduced **only after**: (1) Ad Grants approved,
  running, and stable; (2) counsel confirms entity/data/payment/consent/private-
  benefit/contractor structure; (3) Flow B communication/payment systems are
  approved.

Questions:

- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] How may paid membership levels be structured without private-benefit, inurement, commerciality, or Ad Grants contamination risk?
- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] How should U.S. membership revenue be separated from any Peru-side in-person fees?
- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] What payment routing creates tax, nonprofit, or private-benefit risk, given Embodier.ai operates payments as contractor?
- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] Is any conservation-related giving permissible, and how would it avoid donation-for-access mechanics?

## 6. Conversion definitions (Ad Grants, Flow A)

Count: free PachaMind learning membership created, lesson started, lesson
completed, video watched, quiz completed, reflection submitted, module
completed, badge earned.

Do not count: paid membership interest, upgrade interest, Embodier.ai commercial
contact, screening interest, travel interest, Peru pathway interest.

## 7. Launch gates (what counsel is asked to confirm)

| Gate | Must be confirmed before |
|---|---|
| Free membership signup live | Field set, consent text, and data handling reviewed |
| Optional WhatsApp educational messaging | WhatsApp consent + policy obligations reviewed |
| Flow B activation (Embodier.ai commercial) | Entity, data, payment, consent, private-benefit, contractor structure confirmed; Flow B comms/payment approved |
| Paid membership levels | All of: Ad Grants approved/stable, counsel structure confirmed, Flow B comms/payment approved |
| Peru pathway | Operational, legal, payment, and consent gates cleared |
