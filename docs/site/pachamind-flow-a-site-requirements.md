## Scope

Requirements for building PachaMind.org during Flow A: the free, mission-first,
commercial-free, Google Ad Grants–safe Andean wisdom learning site. This
governs information architecture, the free membership signup, communication
preferences, conversion tracking, and the no-commerce posture.

## Non-negotiable posture

PachaMind.org Flow A must remain:

- Free (free PachaMind learning membership only)
- Educational and mission-first
- Commercial-free
- Bilingual EN/ES where practical
- Mobile-first and low-bandwidth friendly
- Accessible (WCAG AA target)
- Explicitly attributed to Mallku Aribalo / James Arévalo Merejildo as tradition-holder and Andean shamanic teacher

PachaMind.org Flow A must **not** include:

- Donations, donation checkout, donor funnel, or "support us" giving mechanics
- Conservation contribution funnel
- Paid memberships, paid membership levels, or paid upgrades
- Pricing
- Payment forms
- Booking, applications, or travel sales
- Ceremony CTAs
- Embodier.ai sales pages, branding, or commercial opt-in
- Paid SMS or SMS marketing capture
- Commercial lead-generation language
- Public Huachuma/San Pedro conversion hooks
- Health, medical, therapeutic, trauma, addiction, anxiety, depression, PTSD, cure, or outcome claims

## Free membership signup requirements

During Flow A, PachaMind.org may collect free membership signup information for
**educational membership administration only**, subject to counsel review.

Preferred Flow A free membership fields:

| Field | Notes |
|---|---|
| First name | |
| Last name | |
| Email | Primary educational communication channel |
| Country code | Pairs with cell/WhatsApp number |
| Cell / WhatsApp number | For **optional** WhatsApp educational communication only — not paid SMS |
| Communication permissions | **Separate** permissions per channel; do not bundle |

Rules:

- Communication permissions must be **separate** (e.g., email educational
  updates vs. optional WhatsApp educational updates), not a single bundled
  consent.
- A free membership signup is **never** automatic Embodier.ai commercial
  consent. Commercial consent is a separate Flow B product on Embodier.ai
  surfaces.
- No payment fields, no pricing, no upgrade prompts.

- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] Whether collecting cell/WhatsApp number during Flow A is permissible and free of nonprofit lead-generation, data, or Ad Grants risk.
- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] Exact consent language for email and optional WhatsApp educational communication.
- [NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION] WhatsApp Business policy obligations for educational (non-marketing) messaging.

## Communication preferences (Flow A)

- Prefer **email** for educational communication.
- Offer **optional WhatsApp** educational communication.
- **No paid SMS** during Flow A.
- Mobile-first commercial messaging (WhatsApp/SMS before email) belongs to
  Embodier.ai Flow B, after separate commercial consent and counsel-approved
  launch gates.

## Conversion tracking (Ad Grants safe)

Count only free educational actions as Flow A conversions:

- Free PachaMind learning membership created
- Lesson started
- Lesson completed
- Video watched
- Quiz completed
- Reflection submitted
- Module completed
- Badge earned

Do **not** track as Ad Grants conversions: paid membership interest, upgrade
interest, Embodier.ai commercial contact, screening interest, travel interest,
or Peru pathway interest.

Analytics must keep nonprofit educational events separate from any future
Embodier.ai commercial events.
