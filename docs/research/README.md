## How to use this directory

Research here grounds curriculum, attribution, and positioning. Before any
research becomes a public asset, classify it through the
[content classification matrix](../content/pachamind-content-classification-matrix.md)
and check it against the
[Flow A no-commerce checklist](../compliance/flow-a-no-commerce-checklist.md).

Hard rules carried from canon:

- PachaMind.org Flow A stays free, educational, mission-first, commercial-free,
  and Google Ad Grants safe.
- No health/medical/cure/outcome claims.
- No drug-tourism, psychedelic-retreat, Huachuma/San Pedro conversion hooks, or
  public ceremony-access framing.
- Always credit **Mallku Aribalo / James Arévalo Merejildo** as tradition-holder
  and Andean shamanic teacher where relevant.
- Future revenue is framed as **membership / membership levels**, never
  donations. There are no donations, pricing, or payment forms on PachaMind.org
  Flow A.

  and tradition-holder.

  communications, marketing, and sales under counsel-approved agreements; does
