# PachaMind.org Public Copy Style Guide

> **Canon amendment:** June 17, 2026. Aligns all public PachaMind.org copy with
> the [PachaMind Operating Architecture](../strategy/pachamind-operating-architecture.md).
> Future revenue is framed as **membership and membership levels**, never as
> donations. PachaMind.org Flow A has no donations, donor asks, pricing, paid
> upgrades, or payment language.
>
> **Not legal advice.** `[NEEDS COUNSEL/VERIFICATION]` items are open questions
> for qualified advisors.

## Who this is for

Anyone writing public-facing PachaMind.org copy: pages, lessons, email,
optional WhatsApp educational messages, metadata, and Ad Grants landing text.
For internal/gated/research material, see the
[content classification matrix](pachamind-content-classification-matrix.md).

## Voice

- Warm, respectful, plainspoken, educational.
- Mission-first: we exist to teach Andean wisdom and preserve culture, for free.
- Reverent toward the tradition and its holder; never sensational or exoticizing.
- Bilingual EN/ES where practical.

Recurring promise to reinforce: **"No cost, no sales — only learning."**

## Always

- Describe PachaMind as a **free** Andean wisdom education project.
- Offer a **free PachaMind learning membership** as the call to action.
- Credit **Mallku Aribalo / James Arévalo Merejildo** as tradition-holder and
  Andean shamanic teacher wherever the tradition is taught.
- Frame any future revenue as **PachaMind membership** and **membership
  levels**.
- Keep email as the primary educational channel; offer **optional WhatsApp**
  educational updates as a separate choice.

## Never (Flow A public copy)

- "Donate," "donation," "give," "support us," "contribute," "chip in," or any
  donor/donation-funnel language.
- "Conservation contribution," "fund the garden," or any giving-for-conservation ask.
- Pricing, "upgrade," "premium," "pro," "paid plan," "membership fee," or
  payment/checkout language.
- "Book," "reserve," "apply," "spots," "deposit," or travel-sales language.
- Ceremony access, retreat, or in-person "join us in Peru" hooks.
- Huachuma / San Pedro / plant-medicine conversion hooks on public surfaces.
- SMS marketing / "text us to get started" capture (no paid SMS in Flow A).
- Embodier.ai branding, sales, or commercial opt-in.
- Health, medical, therapeutic, trauma, addiction, anxiety, depression, PTSD,
  cure, treatment, diagnosis, or guaranteed-outcome claims.

## Membership language (preferred phrasing)

| Avoid | Prefer |
|---|---|
| "Donate to support PachaMind" | "Create your free PachaMind learning membership" |
| "Become a donor" | "Become a PachaMind member" |
| "Upgrade your plan" | (Not used in Flow A; membership levels are introduced only after Flow B gates) |
| "Contribute to conservation" | "Learn about Andean conservation and cultural preservation" |
| "Premium access" | "Free learning membership" |

When membership levels do exist (Flow B, after gates), describe them as
**membership levels**, valuable as education and community on their own — never
as donations and never as a path to ceremony or travel access.

## Communication copy

- **Email (Flow A primary):** educational updates, lesson nudges, new-content
  announcements. No sales, no upgrade prompts, no donor asks.
- **WhatsApp (Flow A optional):** educational reminders only, after a separate
  opt-in. Not marketing.
- A free membership signup is **never** treated as Embodier.ai commercial
  consent; do not imply that signing up opts a member into marketing or sales.

## Mallku attribution pattern

Where the tradition is taught, include a clear credit, for example:

> Taught in honor of the Andean tradition carried by **Mallku Aribalo (James
> Arévalo Merejildo, Chaski)**, tradition-holder and Andean shamanic teacher.

Avoid: unverified biography, sensational shaman imagery, or any claim of
guaranteed personal transformation.

## Quick self-check before publishing

- Is it free, educational, mission-first, and commercial-free?
- Does it avoid donations, pricing, payments, and upgrades?
- Does it avoid health/outcome claims and ceremony/Huachuma conversion hooks?
- Is Mallku credited where the tradition appears?
- Are communication permissions presented as separate, opt-in choices?
- Could anything read as Embodier.ai commercial consent? If so, rewrite.
