# PachaMind.org Flow A Launch Spec

## Purpose

Flow A is the nonprofit education launch for PachaMind.org. Its job is to build a free, mission-first Andean wisdom learning site that can support Google Ad Grants readiness without becoming a commercial funnel for Embodier.ai or a front-door pathway into Peru experiences.

This spec follows the Space roadmap canon: PachaMind.org first, Embodier.ai commercial bridge second, Peru pathway third.

## Non-negotiable launch posture

PachaMind.org must remain:

- Free
- Educational
- Mission-first
- Commercial-free
- Bilingual EN/ES where practical
- Mobile-first and low-bandwidth friendly
- Accessible to WCAG AA target
- Explicitly attributed to Mallku Aribalo / James Arevalo Merejildo as tradition-holder and Andean shamanic teacher

PachaMind.org must not include:

- Donation checkout
- Paid memberships
- Pricing
- Booking, applications, or travel sales
- Ceremony CTAs
- Embodier.ai sales pages
- Payment forms
- Commercial lead-generation language
- Public Huachuma/San Pedro conversion hooks
- Health, medical, therapeutic, trauma, addiction, anxiety, depression, PTSD, cure, or outcome claims

## Flow A conversion model

Safe nonprofit educational conversions:

- Free account created
- First lesson started
- First lesson completed
- Video watched
- Quiz completed
- Reflection submitted
- Module completed
- Badge earned

Do not count Embodier.ai opt-in, paid membership interest, screening interest, travel interest, or commercial contact as Ad Grants conversions.

## Signup model decision

**Settled by the June 17, 2026 canon (see
[Operating Architecture](pachamind-operating-architecture.md#free-gamified-learning-membership-and-membership-coordinates)).**
Flow A is a free, gamified, two-way learning membership that collects the
approved **membership coordinates** for the real educational product — not a
passive newsletter and not a hidden commercial lead funnel. The earlier
"phone-first vs. email-first vs. dual-field" decision is no longer open.

Approved membership fields: first name, last name, email, country/region,
country code, cell/WhatsApp number, preferred language, and separate per-channel
communication permissions. Email is the account/access channel; WhatsApp is
optional and educational; paid SMS is excluded (optional educational SMS only if
activated later).

Best-case operating determination: the risk is **not** collecting reasonable
membership coordinates; it is using them for undisclosed Embodier.ai commercial
marketing, paid promotion, screening, payment, or Peru-pathway sales before
separate Flow B consent. Counsel focuses on consent wording, logging,
data-sharing, and channel consent.

Implementation stance: build signup against a real educational product
(dashboard, progress, lesson completion, video watch state, reflections,
quizzes, badges, communication preferences, consent logging) so field and
consent wording can be tuned without rebuilding the learning platform.

## First five lessons

1. What PachaMind is: free Andean wisdom education and cultural preservation.
2. Mallku Aribalo: tradition-holder, Andean shamanic teacher, and cultural anchor.
3. Kausay Pacha: the living universe.
4. The Three Pachas: Hanaq, Kay, and Uku as a learning map.
5. The Five Andean Values: Ayni, Munay, Yachay, Llank'ay, Sami and Hucha.

## No-commerce checklist

Before launch, every public page should pass:

- No pricing
- No checkout
- No donation checkout
- No sales CTA
- No booking/application CTA
- No Embodier.ai commercial opt-in
- No public Huachuma/San Pedro conversion hook
- No medical/therapeutic/outcome claim
- Mallku credited where tradition is discussed
- PachaMind educational mission clear
- Footer links live
- HTTPS active
- Mobile speed acceptable
- Analytics separate nonprofit education events from Embodier.ai commercial events
