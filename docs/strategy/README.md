# PachaMind Strategy Canon

This directory is the durable GitHub home for PachaMind research and strategy
artifacts synced from the Perplexity PachaMind Space canon.

## June 17, 2026 canon amendment

The controlling canon is the
[PachaMind Operating Architecture](pachamind-operating-architecture.md). Its key
amendments: future revenue is framed as **membership and membership levels**,
not donations; PachaMind.org Flow A has **no donations, donor funnel,
conservation contribution funnel, paid upgrades, pricing, or payment forms** and
offers **free PachaMind learning memberships only**; Flow A communication is
**email + optional WhatsApp** (no paid SMS); and **Embodier.ai is the
contractor/operator** for memberships, payments, CRM, communications, marketing,
and sales — not the mission owner and not the public educational provider. Where
older documents reference donation checkout, donor, or conservation-contribution
mechanics, the Operating Architecture controls.

## Roadmap canon

The current sequencing is:

1. **Flow A** — PachaMind.org free, commercial-free education and Google Ad
   Grants readiness, first. Free PachaMind learning memberships only.
2. **Flow B** — Embodier.ai commercial mobile-first (WhatsApp/SMS) CRM/membership
   bridge, second, only after Ad Grants are approved/stable, counsel confirms the
   structure, and Flow B comms/payment systems are approved.
3. **Peru-only screened pathway** — third, after operational, legal, payment,
   and consent gates.

Entity separation between **PachaMind** (nonprofit education, mission owner,
public educational provider), **Embodier.ai** (commercial contractor/operator),
**Mallku** (tradition-holder), and Peru contractors is a hard guardrail across
all of these documents.
